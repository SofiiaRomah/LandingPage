OUR STORY

Launched in Ukraine in 2005, Dodger PR Agency has helped dozens of local and international clients develop and implement effective communications campaigns.

Dodger team has extensive experience in a variety of industries, including social projects, energy efficiency, finance, FMCG, IT, telecommunications, automotive, pharmaceutical, tourism and culture.

Our PR campaigns include awareness raising campaigns, government relations and NGOs, anti-crisis PR, corporate and product PR, PR for NGOs and State clients, social media campaigns on the national and regional level in Ukraine and globally.
